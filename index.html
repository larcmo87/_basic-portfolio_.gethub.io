About Me

I work for ADP (Automatic Data Processing) as an Interface Analyst/Developer. I have been employed with ADP for the past 10 years. I have helped to build, design, code and impletement many interfaces for ADP clients. Prior to my employement with ADP I worked for a software developement compay named Sire Technoligies. There we developed an Electronic Document Management System that is used mainy by muncipalities (cities, counties, federal courts, etc...).

My interests include the arts, technology, sports, alomst any outdoor activity and painting.
